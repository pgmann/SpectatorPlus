Sets or deletes the spectator lobby.

The spectator lobby is a location, defined with this
command, where all spectators are teleported when they
enter or leave spectator mode, or when they leave an arena.

§6/spec lobby set§r: sets the lobby location to your
current position. Must be executed as a player.
§6/spec lobby delete§r: deletes the lobby. Players
will no longer be teleported when spectator mode is
enabled or disabled.
