Updates the spectating mode.

The spectating mode represents the way players
are chosen by the teleporter. Three modes are available.

§8- §6ANY§r: spectators can teleport themselves to
any non-spectating player on the server.
§8- §6ARENA§r: spectators must first select an
arena §7(see /spec help arena)§r; then, they will
be able to teleport themselves to any player inside
the selected arena's bounds.
§8- §6WORLD§r: spectators can teleport themselves
to any player in their current world.
