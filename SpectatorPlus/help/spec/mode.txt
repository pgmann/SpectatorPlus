Updates the spectating mode.

The spectating mode represents the way the players
a spectator is able to see in his teleporter are
selected. Three modes are available.

§8- §6ANY§r: the players can teleport themselves to
any non-spectating player in the server.
§8- §6ARENA§r: the players will have to select an
arena §7(see /spec help arena)§r; then, they will
be able to teleport themselves to the player inside
the bound of the selected arena only.
§8- §6WORLD§r: the players can teleport themselves
to any player in the same world.
